SwiftQueue

A before/after dashboard modeling Ticketmaster's 2022 Taylor Swift (Eras Tour) presale queue failure by comparing a naive baseline queue against a linked-list-driven optimized redesign.

BASELINE APPROACH

Array-based random queue

Tickets get a random unique number checked against existing entries. Refreshing the page discards your position and issues a new one, rewarding spam-refresh over fairness.

Open baseline demo → OPTIMIZED APPROACH

Linked-queue with live simulation

A singly linked list used as a FIFO queue. Joining appends to the tail in O(1), and refreshing finds your node again so your real position is always restored.

Open optimized demo →

BaselineOptimized
